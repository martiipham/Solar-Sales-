**For:** Martin Pham (and future sales team)
**Purpose:** Outreach, qualification, and closing process for solar SME clients

---

## Ideal Client Profile (ICP) Quick Reference

**YES signals:**
- Solar installation company, 5–15 salespeople
- Already using GoHighLevel (or open to it)
- Owner-operated or owner-managed
- Mentions "leads going cold", "can't follow up fast enough", "missing jobs"
- Google reviews mention fast response or complain about slow contact
- Running Google Ads or Facebook Ads (spending on leads = lead pain)

**NO signals (for now):**
- Fewer than 3 salespeople (too small to justify cost)
- Enterprise (50+ staff — different buying process)
- Not in Australia
- Just getting started (no lead volume yet)

## Discovery Call Framework (30 Minutes)

### Opening (5 min)
"Before I show you anything, I want to understand your operation a bit. Okay if I ask a few questions?"

### Key Discovery Questions

**Volume and flow:**
- "How many leads do you get per week across all channels?"
- "Where do most come from — Google, Facebook, referrals?"
- "What does a lead look like when it arrives in your system?"

**Pain:**
- "What happens to a lead the moment it comes in — who gets it, how fast do they call?"
- "Have you ever lost a job where you knew the lead was hot but just didn't get to them in time?"
- "What percentage of your leads would you say are actually worth calling?"

**Current setup:**
- "Are you on GoHighLevel? How are you using it?"
- "Do you have any automation set up — follow-up texts, emails, anything?"

**Decision-making:**
- "Is it just you who'd be making this decision, or is there someone else involved?"
- "If we showed you something that worked, what would you need to see to move forward?"

### Pivot to Demo (5 min)
"Based on what you've told me, I think there are two or three things that are directly relevant to you. Can I show you?"

### Demo Flow (15 min)
1. Show lead arriving in GHL (or describe the flow)
2. Show qualification score appearing
3. Show Slack alert firing
4. Play a sample AI call recording
5. Show dashboard with pipeline health

### Handling Objections (5 min)
See objection handler below.

### Close (5 min)
"Based on everything you've seen — is this something that makes sense for your business right now?"

If yes: "Great — the way it works is a $1,500/month retainer. We can have you live in 48 hours. Want to lock in a start date?"

If "I need to think about it": "Totally fair. What's the main thing you want to think through?" → Address it, then re-ask.

## Objection Handler

### "It's too expensive."
"I hear you. Let me put it in context — if this system books you one extra job per month that you would have otherwise lost, what's your average job value?"

[They say $8,000–15,000.]

"So one job covers the retainer for six months. The question isn't really cost — it's whether the system works. And that's why we offer the first month as a trial. If it doesn't measurably improve your pipeline in 30 days, we'll refund it. Does that change the calculus?"

### "We already have a CRM / we do follow-up ourselves."
"That's good — the system doesn't replace what you have, it speeds it up. It still goes into GHL, your team still closes the deals. The AI just makes sure no lead sits cold for more than a few minutes. Does your team currently call every lead within 5 minutes of arrival, even at 9pm on a Friday?"

### "I'm not sure about AI calling customers."
"That's a fair concern. A couple of things: the AI only calls when a human hasn't — it's a backup, not a replacement. And the call recording goes into GHL so your team can listen and follow up. We can also set it to only call during business hours, or always hand off to a human after the opening. Would that make you more comfortable?"

### "We're too busy right now."
"Busy is a good problem. Actually, that's exactly when this pays off most — when leads are coming in fast and your team is stretched, things fall through the cracks. This is designed for that scenario. Setup takes us 48 hours and we handle everything. Your team just keeps doing what they're doing."

### "We've tried automation before and it didn't work."
"What happened? [Listen.] That's common — most automation tools are generic and need a lot of configuration. This is built specifically for solar companies in Australia. The qualification criteria, the call script, the pipeline stages — they're all set up for your industry out of the box."

## Pricing and Packages

### Quoting Guidelines
- Never start below $1,500/month (undermines positioning)
- Lead with $2,000 (Tier 2 with voice AI) — it's the better product
- Offer $1,500 only if they push back on budget
- First-month-free trial for qualified prospects only (not as default)
- Annual pre-pay: 10% discount (2 months free)

### Proposal Format
Send a one-page proposal within 24 hours of a positive discovery call. Include:
1. Their specific pain (from the call)
2. What the system does for them
3. Investment (monthly, annual option)
4. Start date and onboarding steps
5. Call-to-action: "Reply YES to lock in [date]"
